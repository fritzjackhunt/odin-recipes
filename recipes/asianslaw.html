Asian Slaw

Description

A simple healthy recipe for Asian Slaw - easy and crunchy, this vegan slaw can be made ahead and is delicious alongside salmon, tofu, or chicken or stuffed into tacos, topped onto burgers, or added to buddha bowls. A great way to add more veggies into your everyday meals

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Toasted sesame oil
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Maple syrup, honey, brown rice syrup, or agave
  • Soy sauce
  • Fresh garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Salt
  • Chili paste

Steps

  1. Toss slaw ingredients together in a large bowl. Add cilantro and scallions.
  2. Whisk Asian Slaw Dressing ingredients together in a small bowl.
  3. Pour dressing into slaw and toss well.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds and/ or nuts.
